What is this site about?

It is about life of informatics students. Our routine is full of exciting moments, which can be beautiful, scary or just funny. Memes are perfect way to capture these moments and to share with the public.
Instead of posting them in different places on Facebook or Instagram, it's better to make one huge collection in one place.

What to do here?

  1. Choose category
  2. Enjoy
  3. ... or make your own meme (the possible templates are given here) and share it. Just send it to it.ut.memes@gmail.com!

Can I freely use memes from here for my own purposes?

Yes! They are absolutely free to use. By the way, key-point is that when you want something to be posted here, you give agreement on using it by others.
